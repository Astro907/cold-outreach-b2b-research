## Post 2 — The 3-Cluster Email Sequence (Codex 9)
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/michaeljustin_codex-9-the-3-cluster-email-sequence-activity-6903324289068535808-AOcD

A "spear" is a short, compact 3-4 line email focused on:
- Customer outcomes
- Social proof
- Conversational but authoritative language

The 3-cluster sequence structure:
- Cluster 1: Cold spear + call
- Cluster 2: Follow-up bump + call
- Cluster 3: Final bump + breakup

Bump messages are sparse — they direct back to the original thread. This sends open rates through the roof on the main spears.

The JMM cold call flip: you get 3 heartbeats. The prospect should be mainly talking. You can't logic them to death — build trust first, let them talk, then sell. Buyers are liars. Their default is to brush off callers. Plan for it.
