# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS — Research Repository

A structured research project collecting content from 10 practitioners who actively build and run cold outreach systems for B2B SaaS companies. Built as part of the 100Hires Junior Growth Marketing program.

---

## What this repo is

This is not a collection of thought leaders who write about cold email. Every expert here has either:
- Personally sent hundreds of thousands of cold emails
- Built an agency or product that runs outbound at scale
- Used cold outreach as the primary channel to grow their own company

The goal is to collect enough high-signal material to build a real cold outreach playbook for B2B SaaS.

## Why cold outreach pipeline for B2B SaaS

Cold outreach is one of the highest-leverage growth channels for B2B SaaS because it's:
- Fully founder-controllable (no algorithm dependency)
- Highly measurable (reply rate, meeting rate, pipeline created)
- The fastest way to get in front of a specific ICP with zero budget

The material collected here will support building a full outbound playbook covering: ICP definition, list building, copywriting, sequencing, deliverability, and follow-up strategy.
